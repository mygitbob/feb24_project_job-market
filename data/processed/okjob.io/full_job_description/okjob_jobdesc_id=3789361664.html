Job description

As a Senior SEO Consultant, you will be responsible for a portfolio of clients, managing their SEO strategy and deliverables every month. As a valued member of the team, you will be a key figure in helping us shape the company and its growth.

Role responsibilities

  • Defining the SEO and content strategy for a portfolio of clients.
  • Executing these strategies and prioritising activities to best achieve goals.
  • Supporting clients outside your portfolio when requested.
  • Keyword research and analysis to decide how this data can be used to drive organic growth.
  • Conducting technical SEO audits to identify any issues and/or new opportunities.
  • Responding to ad-hoc client requests and prioritising against planned activity.
  • Conducting competitor content gap analysis and using these insights to feed into client strategies.
  • Compiling reports on the organic performance of all assigned clients.
  • Analysing the impact our work has had and writing commentary to summarise to clients.
  • Monitoring SEO performance and flagging any notable shifts (positive or negative).
  • Coming up with creative SEO ideas to help clients achieve their organic search goals.
  • Support with off-site strategy and competitor backlink audits.
  • Leading/joining client meetings and calls.
  • Writing detailed and clear briefs for both internal teammates and client teams.
  • QA work to ensure that all deliverables are of the highest standard.
  • Product innovation and ideation to help us improve processes and procedures.
  • Helping shape the direction and operations of the company as a whole.

About you

You have over 4 years of experience in an SEO role. You have experience working as an SEO professional, preferably in an agency. You have independently completed complex SEO projects. You are methodical in your approach (but avoid paralysis by analysis). You have an execution-driven mindset. You have hands-on experience with content SEO and technical SEO. You are good at project and time management to ensure that accounts are well serviced but not overrun and that deadlines are met. You are a creative problem-solver who is focused on finding solutions. You can accurately log hours spent on each client using our time-tracking software (ClickUp). You keep up to date on SEO news and algorithm updates. You prioritise effectively, take ownership, and get things done. You can comfortably explain reports and complex concepts to clients and important stakeholders. You feel comfortable managing and mentoring junior members of the team and sharing knowledge. You are a clear communicator — both verbal and written. You have excellent attention to detail. You are passionate about SEO and understand the importance of organic search. You work well individually and within a team. You are not afraid to learn new things.

Desired but not necessary skills: you have experience with Python and using AI for SEO purposes.
