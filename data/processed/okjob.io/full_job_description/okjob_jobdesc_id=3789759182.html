The Opportunity

As Senior Account Executive - PR, you will be:

  • Supporting PR programs from beginning to end, working with team members to coordinate and complete each step in the process
  • Acting as point of contact for clients on projects and one-off asks
  • Supporting account leads with planning, strategy, briefs and timelines, maintaining a high quality to your deliverables
  • Staying up-to-date on current news and industry trends impacting our work, sharing POVs internally and externally
  • Participating in mentorship and training programs

About You

Experience you have:

  • You have about 2-3 years of experience in public relations, communications, and/or client service in a related field, in an agency environment.
  • You’ve supported accounts and client workstreams, and have successfully placed coverage in national, local and trade media outlets.
  • You’ve worked on multiple account teams at once and interacted with clients regularly.
  • You’re familiar with consumer brands.

Equally as important, you are:

  • Ambitious and entrepreneurial
  • Flexible and adaptable
  • Meticulous and well organized
  • A creative thinker and problem solver
  • Inquisitive and eager to learn
  • Collaborative and a team player
  • Honest and approach all work ethically
  • Committed to advancing diversity, equity, inclusion and belonging in the workplace
