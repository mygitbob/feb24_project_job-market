About ThredUP

thredUP is transforming resale with technology and a mission to inspire a new generation of consumers to think secondhand first. By making it easy to buy and sell secondhand, thredUP has become one of the world's largest online resale platforms for apparel, shoes, and accessories. Sellers love thredUP because we make it easy to clean out their closets and unlock value for themselves or for the charity of their choice while doing good for the planet. Buyers love shopping value, premium, and luxury brands all in one place, at up to 90% off estimated retail price. Our proprietary operating platform is the foundation for our managed marketplace and consists of distributed processing infrastructure, proprietary software and systems, and data science expertise. With thredUP's Resale-as-a-Service, some of the world's leading brands and retailers are leveraging our platform to deliver customizable, scalable resale experiences to their customers. thredUP has processed over 172 million unique secondhand items from 55,000 brands across 100 categories. By extending the life cycle of clothing, thredUP is changing the way consumers shop and ushering in a more sustainable future for the fashion industry.

How You Will Make An Impact

ThredUp is hiring a Senior Manager of Supply Chain Logistics to lead our quickly evolving transportation program to support our rapid growth. This is a high impact role for the company given our dependence on carriers for individual inbound supply shipments as well as outbound orders. Leveraging world-class in-house industrial engineering, software development, and data analysis, you will have all the resources needed to innovate, test, and manage new supply chain solutions. This position will report to the Director of Supply Chain.

This role is a great fit for you if you're a self-starter with an enthusiasm to become a world-class supply chain strategist with a track record of successful, creative transportation implementations. You excel at collaborating with cross-functional teams and outside vendors to build new transportation solutions and thrive in a data-rich environment that unleashes your relentless first principle thinking. You’re willing to roll up your sleeves and be curious about all aspects of internal and carrier operations in pursuit of the ultimate customer experience. You have the potential to become an expert negotiator who will hustle to deliver cost-effective yet flexible transportation contracts to deliver predictable budget adherence. You think big and enjoy a high-growth, fast-paced environment where you will be resourceful, learning fast, and taking calculated risks.

In This Role You’ll Get To:

  • Manage the development of our own in-house transportation network to consolidate orders and inventory across multiple domestic automated distribution centers
  • Be responsible for our relationship with all logistics partners including inbound and outbound small parcel carriers, freight, 3PL storage, trailer rental, and truck fleet providers
  • Innovate, experiment, and develop creative supply chain solutions with our amazing operations, engineering, and data science teams
  • Oversee the daily end-to-end flow of our small parcel and freight operations across multiple locations
  • Have ownership of a large logistics budget and achieve hero status by reducing inbound and outbound unit costs
  • Apply your First Principles thinking in a data-rich environment to create world-class transportation processes
  • Committed to continuously develop, mentor, and grow direct reports

What We’re Looking For:

  • Experience developing new, creative logistical processes and solutions with cross-functional operations and engineering teams
  • Strong negotiator with deep experience managing and reducing large transportation budgets
  • Solid knowledge and proficiency in spreadsheet modeling
  • Exceptional analytical (you know how to work a spreadsheet) and problem-solving skills (you like to ask why!)
  • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, ever-changing business
  • Possess an entrepreneurial and founder mindset, ready to take on tasks outside of role with strong agility to change and to be flexible/resilient
  • 4+ years of supply chain or logistics experience
  • BA/BS preferred
