Job Description

Simprints is a nonprofit tech company with a mission to radically increase transparency and effectiveness in global development. We build ethical, inclusive digital ID powered by biometrics to verify that health, aid, and services truly reach people. Simprints partners with organizations like Gavi to boost immunization rates in developing countries, Ministries of Health like Ghana on pandemic response, and NGOs like BRAC to deliver maternal healthcare. Studies have shown Simprints increases impact through real-time, precision data, for example increasing maternal health visits by 38% in Bangladesh or accurate HIV tracing by 62% in Malawi. Today we’ve worked in over 17 countries helping deliver health, aid, and finance to >2.5M people. Our goal is to transform the way the world fights poverty, ensuring that every vaccine, every dollar, and every public good reaches the people who need them most.

Role Overview

We’ve just raised new catalytic funding to scale our work. We’re looking for an experienced and ambitious Machine Learning Engineer to join our team during this exciting growth phase. You will be working on novel biometric algorithms, specifically designed to work in the toughest contexts of the planet. You will work in an agile, collaborative team with a commitment to clean code and robust design, along with a strong culture of continuous learning and improvement. If building technology for global health and international development sounds exciting, we want to meet you!

Your impact

At Simprints, we want everyone to feel ownership of the tasks they are working on, starting from your first day. As the first ML engineer on the team, you will get a chance to shape not only your role, but the entire ML function at Simprints. Within the first month, we’ll help you set your environment up, and you will start playing with datasets. By your third month, you will have a good understanding of how our products are used and you’ll start architecting. In six months, you will lead with a high degree of autonomy the design and development for new products and functionality.

Responsibilities:

  • Use and improve existing open source biometric algorithms (e.g. face recognition)
  • Implement ML frameworks on Mobile and Cloud
  • Build training pipelines and infrastructure together with our Backend team
  • Team with our Android and Backend teams to design and develop ML products that work for our context
  • Write robust code that is easy to test and maintain, and runs reliably in challenging setting

Interview Process

To apply:

  1. Submit your application. We will then undertake shortlisting and arrange an initial 10-20 minute call with suitable candidates.
  2. Invitation to complete assessment. Once we’ve received your completed assessment, top-scoring applicants will be invited to a video interview.
  3. Video interview. The 30-60 minute interview is an opportunity to understand your motivations for joining Simprints, assess your cultural and technical fit, and provide you with a chance to ask any questions.
  4. Final interview. This is intended to ensure that Simprints is as good a fit for you as you are for us. You’ll have a chance to meet further members of the team. This should last no longer than 2 hours.
