Job Description: Senior Software Engineer

Stora is hiring a Senior Software Engineer. We’ll be accepting applications until 25th January 2024, with a start date ASAP.

This is an exciting opportunity to join our team to help us on our journey to build the operating system for self storage. There’s lots of impactful development work ahead where you can make your mark.

About the role

As a Senior Software Engineer at Stora you'll focus on our core Rails based platform, assisting in the delivery of our roadmap and the creation of internal tools.

We're looking for an engineer with at least five years experience in Ruby who is happy writing the occasional bit of JavaScript when called for.

Generally, we work best with self-starting team players. People who are happy to take the reins and see a piece of work through to completion.

Your responsibilities include:

  • Collaboratively engaging with the product team to define and refine the scope and requirements for new features.
  • Taking a hands-on approach in coding, while simultaneously leading and overseeing a small team of developers through the implementation process.
  • Writing tests
  • Ensuring the app stays performant as we scale
  • Investigating and fixing bugs
  • Mentoring team members

Day to day you'll be working with:

  • Rails 7
  • PostgreSQL
  • Hotwire
  • ViewComponents, Sass and BEM
  • Sidekiq
  • Stripe Connect and Billing
  • Heroku

Stora is a fully remote company, and this is a remote job. For now, we prefer candidates based in the UK, but will consider anywhere with at least 5 hours of overlap with GMT during a normal working day.

About us

Stora launched in 2020 aiming to revolutionise the self storage industry. Our platform was designed to solve real-life problems that competitors failed to, and as a result we’ve brought on board hundreds of customers from across the global. We’re a fast-growing company who’ve gained a reputation as innovators in this space, and we plan to keep it that way. Our team is small, focused and love to build world-class software.

Benefits & compensation

The annual salary for this position is £70K+ depending on experience. This is for a 4-day work week, working 8 hours per day.

Our benefits are designed to help you lead a healthy life outside of work. That’s why we work a 4-day week and offer flexible working hours.

While this is a remote position we also give you the opportunity to connect in real life too, with an optional team day every Thursday in Belfast, or during one of our two company meet ups each year.

To help you do your best work, we provide you with a laptop of your choice and a WFH budget to create your ideal work environment.

Beyond this we offer private health insurance for you and your direct family members, a company pension, 28 days paid annual leave per year and share options.

What happens next?

Should you progress to the interview phase, you'll hear from us within 2 weeks of the deadline. Anticipate 2-3 remote interviews, each lasting 30–60 minutes, with our principal engineers scheduled at your convenience. We will discuss your background, approach, and professional experience. Nothing too crazy - we just want to get a feel for your experience and an idea of how you think you may be able to help us build the next phases of Stora.

Following the interviews, our final candidates may be asked to complete an at-home project that should take no more than four hours. This exercise reflects the nature of our work and enables us to better evaluate your capabilities.
