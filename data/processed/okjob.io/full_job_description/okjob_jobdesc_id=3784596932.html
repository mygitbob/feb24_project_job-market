Job Description

Working within Recruitment part of the business, you will engage with both passive and active candidates to “sell” the benefits of the position you are resourcing. A variety of methods and tools are available, with the aim to find suitable candidates to fulfil the job briefs provided by our client. You will own a number of roles and work directly with the line manager to agree the brief, find the candidate and manage both candidate and client through the entire recruitment process.

  • Identification of potential sources of candidates
  • Location of and interaction with potential candidates pertinent to the client to build talent pools, provide market intel and a pipeline for succession planning and future roles
  • Draw up bespoke search lists identifying target companies and candidates for our client
  • Development and maintenance of good relationships with candidates to enhance the reputation of Curve and our client
  • Network extensively and effectively with colleagues, contacts and candidates to understand the market
  • Recognise the specific outcomes sought by the client, having understood or even identified the issues the client is seeking to address
  • Act as a trusted advisor and be seen as an industry expert to support Curve’s and the client’s recruitment roadmap to fulfil roles as quickly as possible within time to hire KPI’s
  • At times brief other team members and support them in finding suitable candidates
  • Produce management information as required by Curve or clients
  • Screening candidates to ensure suitability for the requirement
  • Demonstrate and confidently use all search tool available to maximise each opportunity
  • Keep abreast on latest/changing resource technology

Qualifications

  • Demonstrable experience in a Recruitment delivery role
  • An excellent telephone manner
  • Determined & driven with a will to succeed
  • Ability to respond to requirements and act with urgency
  • Ability to deliver a persuasive sales message on behalf of our clients
  • Ability to engage with candidates in a professional way, demonstrating market and client knowledge
  • Experience in successfully sourcing commercial Candidates across corporate Head Office roles
  • The intellect, flexibility and robust necessary to overcome difficulties
  • Has a flexible approach and willing to work outside the normal working hours as required, understanding that candidates are not always available to talk within standard office hours
  • Bring alive existing established contacts to enhance and continue to build a network of contacts, pipelining for the future
  • The role may involve handling sensitive data and therefore pre-screening / vetting will be required. Any offer will be subject to these background checks
