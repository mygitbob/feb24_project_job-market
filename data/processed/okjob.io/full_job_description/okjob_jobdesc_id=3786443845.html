About Scoro

Scoro is work management software built for service businesses that are tired of disjointed systems, unpredictable workloads, and shrinking margins. It helps to streamline work and eliminate routine tasks to ensure a business runs as smoothly and efficiently as possible – from quote to cash.

Thousands of teams in more than 60 countries trust Scoro to increase their margins, save time, and accelerate data-driven decision-making. Unlike many single-purpose solutions, Scoro is scalable across an entire business – from projects and sales to billing and reporting.

Scoro’s biggest office is in Tallinn, Estonia, with additional locations in the UK, Latvia, and the US. Scoro has been listed in Deloitte’s Technology Fast 50 list and the Inc. 5000 list.

Role intro

Join Scoro’s marketing team as our next Paid Marketing Manager.

In this role, you will be responsible for paid media and performance marketing programs. You will manage a 6-digit budget and ensure it is spent effectively and efficiently. Your goal is to capture leads ready to buy and support demand generation efforts to build awareness among our target audience.

You will collaborate closely with other Marketing team members, especially our Content and Product Marketing and Sales and Revenue Operations teams.

To be successful, you must have in-depth knowledge of the various paid marketing channels and technologies, including:

  • paid search (Google, Bing)
  • retargeting (LinkedIn, Meta, Google Display Ads)
  • review sites (e.g., G2, Capterra)
  • social network advertising (LinkedIn)
  • content distribution (LinkedIn)

We'd love to hear from you if you are analytical, results-oriented, collaborative, have a go-get attitude, and are passionate about experimenting with different approaches/ideas.

Your responsibilities include:

  • Strategic Planning:
    • Collaborating with cross-functional teams to align digital campaigns with overall business objectives
    • Working closely with the marketing and sales team to generate qualified sales leads and opportunities that drive the pipeline
    • Analyzing the performance of different paid channels and verticals through in-depth analysis, and recommending improvements in areas such as budget allocation, content strategy, and targeting
    • Collaborating with our Google Ads agency to ensure optimal budget spend and results
    • Working closely with our Content team to ensure we have relevant content and creatives for the campaigns. Continuously providing feedback on the performance of content and messaging to optimize campaign effectiveness
    • Creating and managing retargeting campaigns in different channels (LinkedIn, Meta, Google Display ads)
    • Creating and managing demand generation campaigns on LinkedIn (from a content distribution perspective) to build awareness in the target market
    • Gathering insight from demand gen campaigns and using it to inform our ABM team on the most engaged high-value prospects
    • Experimenting with new ideas - proactively exploring and testing new channels, tools, segments, creatives, etc.
