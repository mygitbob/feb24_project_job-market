We are looking for a detailed-oriented individual to support Sales operations

This position will work across the GTM (Go-To-Market) teams (e.g. Sales, CS, Marketing, Business Development, etc.), with exposure to other cross-functional teams (e.g. Legal). This role offers potential opportunities for advancement and development, and high-performing individuals may be considered for full-time employment based on their performance and alignment with the company’s goals and needs. This is a contract position.

What you’ll do:

  • Be responsible for closing out deals for new business, upsells, and renewals in our CRM system (Salesforce.com).
  • Review and execute requests for account assignment requests, segment/territory changes, and opportunity-related inquiries.
  • Help facilitate general Rules of Engagements across all account/lead/opportunity management.
  • Assist in lead management when appropriate (e.g. manual lead routing or lead hygiene).
  • Monitor and take proactive measures against data hygiene and inefficient processes (e.g. closed won opportunities with no billing address, accounts owned by inactive employees, etc.).
  • Own the RevOps process for Onboarding/De-boarding GTM employees in JIRA. This involves coordinating with various stakeholders to ensure a smooth onboarding/deboarding process.
  • Pull basic reports in SFDC (i.e. bookings, account and opportunity ownership, activities, etc.) to help with related operational tasks.
  • Assist in adhoc tickets and projects – specifically focused on tasks that touch upon data hygiene, data validation, data enrichment, and data preparation/transformation.

Qualifications:

  • Minimum of 1 year using Salesforce.com (SFDC) and a power user of Salesforce Lightning. (Required)
  • Experienced with Google Sheets and Excel, with beginner-level knowledge on formulas, functions, and shortcuts (e.g. VLOOKUP, basic mathematical functions, IF statements, data manipulation, sorting/filtering, etc.). (Required)
  • At least 1 year of experience using data tools/applications like Dataloader.io to mass update, create, and delete records in SFDC.
  • Comfortable with Google Suites applications like Gmail, Google Calendar, and Google Drive
  • Previous experience using a project/task management system (i.e. Jira, Monday.com, Wrike). Ability to create tickets, update ticket status, respond to comments, add/link child tickets, and usage of other basic features.
  • Previous experience using a project documentation tool (e.g. Confluence). Ability to create Confluence pages, edit and navigate effectively through the RevOps Confluence space.
  • Familiar with sales intelligence tools like ZoomInfo, EtailInsights, Similarweb, LinkedIn, etc., and the ability to search for records and pinpoint necessary data.
  • Strong attention to detail, especially in identifying data inconsistencies and errors.
  • Strong documentation and communication skills (both written and verbal).
  • Strong bias towards problem solving, execution of work, and learning.
  • A resourceful self-starter that can work independently with minimal supervision.
  • Ability to work cross functionally between departments and sales functions to quickly gather alignment and consensus.

Compensation: $25.00 - $33.00 per hour
