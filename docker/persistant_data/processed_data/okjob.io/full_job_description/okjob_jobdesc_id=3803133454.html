Senior Product Manager

Hutch are looking for a Senior Product Manager to join their team in London. You should be result-oriented and enjoy working with a high level of autonomy. You should have a good knowledge of the mobile gaming market, an understanding of business ethics and a data-driven approach to optimisation. You'll be reporting to the Game Director and work closely with the Analyst and other Product Managers to execute a product-centered strategy that will drive sustainable growth across all KPI's.

Responsibilities

  • Understand and improve internal processes
  • Sole Owner of Live-ops
  • Pricing In-game Assets
  • Collaboration with Analysts & Game Designer
  • Balancing game economy
  • Feature Design
  • Designing offers for improving the monetisation of the game
  • Create hypothesis and a/b test those hypothesis
  • Develop feature ideas that address user and business needs and work cross-functionally (with engineers, UI designers, community, marketing, etc.) to launch them
  • Handling continual product requirements for sustaining the product's health
  • Analyse and report on product performance
  • Deliver market & competitor analyses, with a particular focus on games operating in the same space

Requirements

  • Experience in the mobile gaming industry in a product management or game design role
  • Understanding of A/B testing fundamentals
  • Exceptional analytical skills, including the ability to extract data
  • Understanding of Power of Live-Ops
  • Ability to write clear and concise specs for new features
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills
  • Passion for games and mobile gaming
  • Good written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to work with different stakeholders
  • Interest in "Free to Play" gaming

Bonus Points

  • Ability to create UI wireframes and tailor good UX
  • Experience with CRM tools like Braze, Leanplum etc.
  • Extensive market knowledge (e.g. all top grossing games)
  • Ability to extract data via SQL
  • Motorsports enthusiast
  • Interested in community management
