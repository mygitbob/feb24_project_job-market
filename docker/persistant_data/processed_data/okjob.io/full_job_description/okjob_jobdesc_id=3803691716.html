Purpose of Position

As our Marketing Assistant, you will be an integral member of Awin’s Global Marketing team. Your core responsibilities focus on providing timely and high-quality support to our local Marketing Managers on their respective projects. This role offers the opportunity to touch on many different marketing disciplines and channels (social media, blog content, events, website, e-mail marketing, SEO and SEA) providing a great opportunity to acquire marketing knowledge and insight into how Awin’s global marketing team operates across 11 markets to maintain and strengthen its brand positioning as well as generate new business through multiple acquisition channels. Reporting to the Head of International Marketing, this is the perfect role for someone looking to build their career in marketing.

Key Tasks

  • Support content management by coordinating and publishing blog and social media content created by the team
  • Support with the creation of accompanying imagery via our design request process
  • Collaborate with Marketing Managers and the in-house Creative Design team to produce, update and refresh online marketing assets and offline marketing collateral
  • Monitor and respond to social media enquiries
  • Support with project management of bigger campaigns and events
  • Support with SEO administrative tasks
  • Support with event and merchandise administrative tasks
  • Support with research and reporting tasks
  • Manage marketing email inboxes
  • Liaise with European colleagues and support the regional Marketing Managers with ad-hoc requests

Skills & Expertise

Professional experience:

  • Minimum six months experience as an intern or working student, preferably in a marketing role
  • Fluent in English, additional European languages a welcome bonus
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite and social media platforms
  • Working knowledge of Google Analytics or similar web reporting tools is beneficial
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite and social media platforms

Personal skills:

  • Organised and very detail oriented
  • Self-motivated with the ability and desire to quickly acquire new skills
  • An enthusiastic team player that is happy to get involved and support with whatever is needed
  • Passion for communications and marketing
