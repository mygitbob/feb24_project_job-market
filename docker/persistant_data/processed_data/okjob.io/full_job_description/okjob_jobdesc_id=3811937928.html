About the Job

Kickstarter is the go-to crowdfunding platform for creative projects across everything from film, games, and music to art, design, and technology. Fueled by the support of our team, our mission to help bring creative projects to life has been a core tenet since our inception in 2009.

As of November 2023, we have helped more than 249,000 creative projects reach their funding goal with pledges totaling more than $7.6 billion dollars. We continue to be committed to the growth and success of not only our community, but our team as well. Rooted in shared values, Kickstarter sees team members as whole people, giving every individual the autonomy and space to participate in decisions that impact them.

Job Description

  • Location: United States
  • Salary Range: $115,000 to $130,000

Kickstarter is seeking an experienced People Business Partner to join our People team. In this role, you will work directly with the VP of People, and alongside the People, Finance, and Legal team to drive an improved employee and manager experience. This role will play a critical role in defining and shaping our people programs, performance management process, employee relations, and problem-solving to enhance the employee experience. We are seeking someone who is passionate, dynamic, decisive, has an opinion and always veers on the side of doing the right thing. In this role, you will support 20+ managers across our business.

4 day week

Responsibilities

  • Act as an advisor to our management team to ensure the team’s and business needs are met.
  • Develop and implement HR strategies and programs aligned with the overall business strategy and department goals.
  • Advise and support employee training, employee development, probations, performance improvement plans, and other related matters.

About You

  • At least 5 years of professional experience as an HR business partner at a tech or entertainment company.
  • Experience working in a unionized environment is a plus.
