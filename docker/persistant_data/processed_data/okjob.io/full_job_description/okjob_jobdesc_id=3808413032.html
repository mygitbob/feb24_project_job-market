We are Reflect Digital, a digital marketing agency that is currently looking for an exceptional Project Manager to support the delivery teams deliver high quality work for various client accounts across our digital marketing channels. We are seeking a candidate who has passion for and experience in managing projects within the digital marketing industry and has the ability to build strong relationships with colleagues and clients.

The successful Project Manager will have experience working in a digital marketing agency. They will be a highly organised individual and will have worked on projects using an agile and waterfall approach.

Key responsibilities:

  • Manage the day to day of digital marketing projects and monthly retainers through the use of Productive platform
  • Ensure all work is delivered within budget
  • Develop support documentation including risk logs and requirement specifications
  • Resolve issues promptly
  • Monitor and report on appropriate metrics
  • Lead evaluation and implementation of internal and change management processes
  • Develop a detailed project plan and monitor its progress
  • Create and maintain account planners and road maps
  • Communicate project progress with all stakeholders
  • Ensuring schedule of works are fulfilled
  • Identifying and implementing KPIs and project milestones
  • Contribute to effective client communication where appropriate
  • Analyse success of client work, creating reports and data analysis as supporting evidence
  • Forming strong relationships with clients and developing communication channels between relevant parties
  • Enhancing collaboration between internal teams

What success looks like:

  • High levels of collaboration and efficiency are evident in all areas of work in the agency
  • Strong relationships with clients are formed where they feel informed and updated with project and retainer updates
  • The quality of work produced by the agency continues to maintain a high standard
