Job Description: Growth Marketing Manager EMEA

Signifyd is seeking a Growth Marketing Manager EMEA to join our EMEA team and report to the Director of Financial Services and EMEA Marketing. You will be a key player in our performance advertising efforts, across paid search, display ads, account-based marketing (ABM) for our rapidly growing region.

Your role combines buyer journey knowledge, engagement strategy, and campaign execution to activate a wide range of target segments. Our market (D2C eCommerce) is both broad and deep - we serve thousands of customers, from startups (via partners like Shopify and BigCommerce) to household names like Samsung, Mango, Phillips, Watchshop and Elemis to name a few. You will have the unique market tailwind and the career opportunity of scaling a multi-segment GTM engine.

The ideal candidate will possess both hands-on campaign execution and tools knowledge around performance advertising and ABM and the analytical skills to optimize campaigns based on A/B testing and ongoing results. They should also have a demonstrated ability to be entrepreneurial and independently ideate on campaign improvement.

Key Responsibilities

  • Partner with Director of Financial Services EMEA Marketing in creating campaign, content, email and social media marketing calendar for the EMEA region.
  • Support event sponsorship via digital channels targeting named accounts.
  • Own and manage pipeline generation via paid media and ensure quarterly targets are met.
  • Plan, project-manage, and execute all SEO/SEM, and account-targeted emails for ABM marketing campaigns in Europe, with the view to:
    • Identify trends and insights and optimize campaigns based on the insights.
    • Write copy, execute and support the ongoing maintenance of account landing pages and email campaigns.
    • Brainstorm new and creative growth strategies through digital marketing and ABM.
    • Deliver performance advertising components of strategic account activation campaigns, collaborating across Demand Generation, Business Development, and other teams.
    • Build and report in collaboration with Rev- Ops on campaign ROI, on-site conversions, cost & click performance, impressions/total reach, website traffic, sales funnel lead progression.
    • Support ABM motion and campaign execution within key tools: Google Adwords, Google Analytics, Salesforce, 6Sense, ReachDesk, PathFactory, Drift, Sales loft, etc.
    • Provide ongoing actionable insights into campaign performance to relevant stakeholders.
    • Own reporting and analytics on marketing efficiency and drive ongoing optimization of Demand Gen based on data.
    • Be the day-to-day contact for digital agency ensuring all campaigns are executed on time and on budget.

Required Qualifications:

  • 3-5 years of experience across B2B and ABM marketing.
  • 2+ years working in a startup or high-growth environment.
  • Experience with Google Analytics goal conversions, UTM parameters.
  • Proven track record of owning campaign spend and optimizing return on ad spend.
  • Comfortable owning a pipeline target for your campaign channels.
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills are a must, as well as the ability to work effectively across internal and external organisations.
  • Possess exceptional problem-solving, analytical, and research skills.
  • Proficient with utilization of Salesforce and Marketo.

Nice to haves:

  • Experience in the eCommerce ecosystem or marketing retail technology is a plus.
  • Business proficiency in any European language.
