The Role

We are currently looking for senior/lead frontend developers who are really interested in pushing the limits of what a ReactJS PWA (Progressive Web App) can do.

The product you will be creating will be the digital backbone of a construction organization. It will be used by people who are building our roads, railways, buildings, power & water supplies. We are improving the civilisation we live in by digitizing the very process of building it!

Our platform is meant to be used out in the field, office, and board room. It will help people do their jobs by digitising workflows at the coalface and feeding information up to descriptive and predictive analytics.

For this role, an ideal candidate would be great at:

  • Writing and talking in English, it is the main language we communicate and collaborate in, it would be awesome if your English Level is B or Higher. Please submit your CV and cover letter in English (Required)
  • Writing and understanding Typescript / JavaScript. You have more than 3 years of professional experience using React or Next.js (Required)
  • Making tests a priority by writing tests (unit and functional) and creating testable code (Required)
  • Using CSS pre/post-processors like Tailwind, Post CSS, Styled Components, LESS, SASS and/or others (Required)
  • Semantic documents using HTML, developing great user interfaces (Desirable)
  • Identifying bottlenecks, using tools to diagnose and refactor code (Desirable)
  • Going full stack and, depending on the project, either diving in the Rails services to add a new endpoint or modify behaviour (Desirable)

If you don’t think you meet all the criteria above but still are interested in the job, please apply! Nobody checks every box, and we’re looking for someone excited to join the team!

Applying properly!! Important!!!

We get thousands of applications so please write us a letter as part of your application explaining:

  • Why do you want to join us?
  • What do you think you bring to the team?

We aren't a giant faceless corporation; we really care about the people who want to join our team so all letters will be read thoroughly.

After that, we will reach out to you to schedule a casual conversation to help you to know us a bit better and tell us a bit about yourself.

After that, we will schedule a couple of technical and personal assessments to understand what your level of capability is based on our own development matrix.
