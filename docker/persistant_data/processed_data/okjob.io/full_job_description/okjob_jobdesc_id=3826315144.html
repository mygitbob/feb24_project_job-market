Summary

Join us as a Senior Account Executive at Wonderlic, where your role is more than sales—it's about transformation and setting new benchmarks. In this pivotal position, you'll lead the charge in expanding our client base for Wonderlic Select and Wonderlic Develop, focusing on acquiring our largest new clients. Your expertise in new-logo sales will not only drive revenue growth but also shape the future of our sales strategy. By forging strong relationships with key decision-makers and harnessing your exceptional sales and negotiation skills, you'll play a crucial role in introducing Wonderlic's innovative talent solutions to new markets, and your ability to simplify complex concepts and provide tailored solutions will be crucial in winning over new clients. Together, let's embark on a journey to introduce Wonderlic to new markets, forge successful partnerships, and drive innovation and prosperity for both our clients and products!
