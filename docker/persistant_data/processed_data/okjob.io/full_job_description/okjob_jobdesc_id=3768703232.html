Job Summary:

Assists the Project Manager in producing quality construction documents in an efficient and profitable manner. Transforms initial schematic design by utilizing our custom AutoCAD system to produce working documents.

Responsibilities:

  • Uses AutoCAD, Revit, and manual processes to produce construction documents using instructions and pre-established guidelines set by others.
  • Follows applicable guidelines for project standards.
  • Converts limited verbal and written directions from Job Captain or Project Manager throughout the design process and into a finished product or document.
  • Drafts specification changes as needed.
  • Generates architectural plans and graphics.

Skill Sets:

  • Has knowledge of commonly used architectural concepts, practices, and procedures
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and meet deadlines
  • AutoCAD experience required
  • Revit experience required
  • Design, graphic and related computer skills a plus

Education:

Associate degree in Drafting and/or Design. Preferably has experience in architecture and/or construction.
