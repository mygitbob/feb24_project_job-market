Job Description

As a PR manager, you will be responsible for a portfolio of clients, managing their PR and communication strategy and deliverables every month. As a senior member of the team, you will also be a key figure in helping us shape the company and its growth.

Role Responsibilities

  • Defining PR strategy for a portfolio of clients.
  • Executing these strategies and prioritising activities to best achieve goals.
  • Supporting clients outside your portfolio when requested.
  • Providing ad hoc assistance to other members of the team when requested.
  • Planning and managing outreach and PR campaigns for clients.
  • Writing and sending out outreach emails on behalf of clients.
  • Conducting backlink audits to assess the backlink success of a PR campaign/project.
  • Responding to ad-hoc client requests and prioritising against planned activity.
  • Analysis of competitors’ PR techniques and using insights to feed into client strategies.
  • Compiling reports on PR performance for all assigned clients.
  • Monitoring PR campaign performance and flagging any notable shifts (positive or negative).
  • Coming up with creative ideas to help clients achieve their PR goals.
  • Writing professional and well-structured press releases.
  • Mentoring junior members of the team around digital PR and reputation management.
  • Leading/joining client meetings and calls as a PR senior.
  • QA work to ensure that all deliverables are of the highest standard.
  • Product innovation and ideation to help us improve internal processes and procedures.
  • Helping shape the direction and operations of the company as a whole.

About You

You have over 4 years of experience in a PR role. You have experience working as a senior PR professional, preferably in an agency. You have independently managed successful PR campaigns. You are methodical in your approach (but avoid paralysis by analysis). You have a results-driven mindset. You have hands-on experience with content, social media, SEO, and digital PR. You are good at project and time management to ensure that accounts are well serviced but not overrun and that deadlines are met. You are a creative problem-solver who is focused on finding solutions. You can accurately log hours spent on each client using our time-tracking software (ClickUp). You keep up to date on PR, content, social media, and SEO news & algorithm updates. You prioritise effectively, take ownership, and get things done. You feel comfortable managing and mentoring junior members of the team and sharing knowledge. You are very much a ‘people person’ and enjoy building relationships with clients. You are a clear communicator — both verbal and written. You have excellent attention to detail. You work well both individually and as part of a team. You are not afraid to learn and try new things. You're keen to build a service line within an agency and take ownership of its growth and performance within the business.
