Product Manager

We are looking for an experienced Product Manager, to partner with our teams and represent the business perspective of our games, impacting all stages of development, from ideation to publishing.

During the early stages of development, you will partner with our teams, helping set the strategic direction for our games as well as developing and executing the product vision from the very beginning. As well as helping define business cases and creating forecasts that aid in studio decision-making.

Post Launch, you will be responsible for defining in-game sales, offers, and promotions to maximize revenue and engagement for all of our titles. You'll take charge of the live operations calendar and collaborate with the design team to create live events that increase player engagement and monetization. As well as partnering with the Analytics team to monitor key metrics, such as monetization, game economy, and player engagement, to provide actionable recommendations to enhance the player experience.
