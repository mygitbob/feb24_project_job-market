Would you like to start building up your career in a full-remote, energizing and international environment? If you’re passionate about Affiliate Marketing, digital business models and have a data-driven mindset, then keep reading the offer below!

We are looking for a Junior Commercial Operations Specialist

To undertake the monitoring of the commercial operations for the UK, German, and Polish markets. Therefore, as a Junior Commercial Operations Specialist, you will be responsible for tracking and keeping a healthy and up-to-date status of our post-sales financial activity. This position will report to the Head of Partnerships of Region North-East and in very close collaboration with the Accounting Team and our Business Intelligence department.

Your Field of Activity

  • Monitor commercial agreements with Affiliate Networks and customers
  • Supervise cash and payment flows
  • Report revenue status, deviations, and forecasts to the Regional Sales Manager as well as to the Accounting team
  • Collaborate closely with the UK, German, and Polish-based sales teams
  • Work closely with the Accounting and Business Intelligence team

About You

  • You have an academic background in Business Administration, Digital Marketing, or similar
  • You have a professional background in roles related to Processes, Operations, Business Intelligence or Accounting
  • You are passionate about data analytics
  • You have excellent communication skills and are fluent in English
  • You are willing to work in a fast-paced environment
  • You have an open-minded, hands-on, and self-organized attitude which is positive, enthusiastic, and empathetic
  • You have high motivation and passion to develop with attention to detail and perseverance
  • You have great communication and problem-solving skills
  • You are proactive and enjoy making things happen
