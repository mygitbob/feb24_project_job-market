At Digible, Inc. we love to celebrate our diverse group of hardworking employees – and it shows. We pride ourselves on our collaborative, transparent, and authentic culture. These values are pervasive throughout every step of a Digible employee's journey. We value diversity and believe forming teams in which everyone can be their authentic self is key to our success. We encourage people from underrepresented backgrounds and different industries to apply. Come join us and find out what the best work of your career could look like here at Digible.

The Role:

We are looking for an engineering leader to support the Engineering team responsible for our core products and solutions. You will play a crucial role in leading and shaping the team, setting direction and roadmap, and being a key member of our leadership team.

The role requires a mix of strategic and tactical skills; setting the team up for success through the growth phase into the maturity phase, partnering with Product, and the ability to lead through influence across the organization.

As a team, we come from many different backgrounds, countries, and fields of education. We strongly believe this diversity and agility has helped us build an inclusive and empathetic environment, and we are looking forward to adding your perspective!

You’ll love this job if you:

  • Have a customer-first mentality
  • Create high performing teams focused on solving business problems
  • Want your stakeholders to rave about your team
  • Celebrate watching your team learn, grow, and succeed against ambitious goals
  • Enjoy digging into the technical details

What you’ll do:

  • Build strong relationships with your stakeholders
  • Foster a customer focus and Product Mindset within the team
  • Mentor and coach a team of 10 engineers, facilitating and championing their growth
  • Engage and retain a high performing engineering team
  • Ensure successful delivery against the team’s commitments
  • Organize and oversee engineering roadmaps and key projects
  • Influence technical designs and ensure alignment with strategic goals
  • Drive organization-wide improvements to our process and engineering culture

You should have:

  • 8-10+ years of engineering experience, with at least 3 years in management roles
  • Experience in both small (50-150) and medium-to-large (500+) companies, demonstrating the ability to adapt to dynamic environments
  • Proven track record in leading product engineering teams and collaborating effectively with Product Managers and Designers
  • Strong management skills, with experience in hiring and performance management
  • Outcome-oriented approach, aligning team happiness with business outcomes
  • Ability to identify and focus on areas of greatest impact for the team and business
  • Excellent communication skills, high emotional intelligence; adept at adapting style to different audiences
  • Experience with software design and architecture, with the ability to anticipate and proactively address potential issues with proposed designs
