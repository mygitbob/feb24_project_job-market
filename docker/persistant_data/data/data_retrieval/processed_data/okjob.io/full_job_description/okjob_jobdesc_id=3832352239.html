About Us

We are Laminar Projects!

If you have already heard about us, you are probably reading this because you are tired of the typical old-fashioned and chaotic ways of doing things in construction. But in case you haven’t: we are a project engineering consultancy and tech startup hybrid! We do things the Laminar Way, that is, we bring control and clarity to projects by implementing pragmatic data-driven solutions using a mix of latest technology, our own processes, and a big dose of common sense.

The Role

We are looking for enthusiastic, inspiring and committed people with Data and Digital experience to join our growing team so we can continue making an impact on Engineering projects.

As a dynamic and tech-driven startup, curiosity and ingenuity are two of the main traits that we love about our applicants!

We offer you the opportunity to define, shape and expand your career alongside a cutting-edge team with a unique combination of industry domain and digital expertise which actively allow us to offer solutions to truly address our customer challenges. You will be working in one of the most exciting parts of our business: shape and deliver top notch digital solutions to our customers who struggle implementing these projects themselves.

As Digital Consultant at Laminar you would focus on:

  • Supporting our clients to understand their needs and biggest pain points by unlocking the value of data on projects.
  • Improving business processes, approached with a data & digital technology mindset.
  • Supporting our multi-discipline teams to implement digital solutions that help people to perform their job more effectively and bring long-term value to the project.
  • Supporting and assisting management team in shaping strategies, adopting new ways of working and accelerating progress in unlocking the value of data and digital tools.
  • Liaising with business development activities to help us bring new, innovative and differentiating digital solutions to our clients.
  • Helping us build, develop and retain a more diverse and inclusive team, while developing internal processes so we can scale.

As a candidate, you should have:

  • Degree educated in one of the following: Engineering, Surveying, Project Management, or similar.
  • 4+ years experience in the engineering/construction of large capital projects (>USD 100m).
  • 2+ years experience in data and/or digital process implementation across an organisation or large project.
  • Knowledge of the latest digital tools and processes across project life cycle from design through operations.
  • Great communication skills, able to translate complex ideas in a clear and concise manner to a wide variety of audiences.
  • Demonstrable passion for digital transformation and improving the delivery of Capital Projects.
  • Team player able to work effectively and inclusively with remote teams bringing together our organisation’s vision.
  • Have an analytical mindset and be able to create structure and clarity from uncertainty.

It would also be great if you have:

  • Chartered in relevant body (e.g. ICE, RICS) or moving towards chartership.
  • ISO 19650 certified. PMP/APM membership and/or MBA.
  • Understanding and practical insight of BIM, and Information Management processes.
  • Consultancy experience
  • Product Management/Software development experience
  • Experience with BI tools (e.g. Power BI).

At laminar we are dedicated to build a diverse, inclusive and authentic workplace, so If you don’t think you meet all the criteria but still are excited about this role we encourage you to apply anyways! Nobody checks every box and you may be just the right candidate for this or other roles.

  • You will be supported to excel in whichever areas you are most interested in! With Laminar, development is unbounded.

Application
