Job Description

PDQ, founded in Salt Lake City, UT, makes device management simple, secure, and Pretty Damn Quick. IT teams use our products to reduce complexity, improve efficiency, and enhance control in their unique environments. We are backed by TA Associates and Berkshire Partners, top-tier global PE companies. SimpleMDM and SmartDeploy are powered by PDQ.

PDQ's Mission:

We make device management simple, secure and pretty damn quick.

PDQ's Core Values:

  • Honesty
  • Ownership
  • Collaboration
  • Improvement

In this role, you will support the Sales organization by overseeing processes and metrics directly related to customer retention, expansion, and churn. You will work alongside PDQ sales leadership, Account Managers and Retention Specialists to plan and implement strategies that reduce customer churn through proactive pain point identification and fostering customer loyalty.

How you'll spend your day:

  • Planning data-led churn reduction tactics for retention focused team members
  • Joining calls with team members to offer support and coaching when attempting to win-back or retain customers
  • Creating automated processes for the team to address accounts with churn indicators
  • Assisting and training the team on creating upsell and cross-sell offers that directly address known customer pain points
  • Analyzing churn reasons, creating an offboarding process and checklist, and conducting offboarding interviews to determine why customers are leaving; sharing these insights with cross-functional stakeholders (e.g. product, marketing, etc.)
  • Motivating & managing a team that’s flexible to shift strategies as new data / opportunities become available
  • Reporting regularly on sales activities, sales pipeline and progress towards GRR and NRR
  • Oversee team and coach 1:1 on effective pipeline management strategies, execution of tasks, email improvements, closing tactics, overcoming obstacles, etc.
  • Stay updated on market trends, competitor activities, and industry developments

We're looking for people who have:

  • 4+ years of SaaS/B2B customer success or sales experience, consistently meeting or exceeding targets; preferrable 1-2 of those years with experience managing a CSM, AM, AE, or BDR/SDR team
  • Strong background in managing the execution of high-output teams utilizing efficient processes
  • Firm grasp of CRM software (Salesforce highly preferred), as well as Microsoft Office (Outlook, Excel, Word and PowerPoint)
  • Proven track record of learning new SaaS products
  • Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration or a related degree, or equivalent experience

Who you are:

  • Ownership: You take responsibility for projects, drive results, and deliver on commitments
  • Honesty: You demonstrate integrity, transparency, and ethical behavior in all interactions
  • Collaboration: You work effectively with cross-functional teams and foster a culture of teamwork
  • Improvement: You continuously seek opportunities for growth, innovation, and personal development
  • Strong organizational
  • Excellent mentoring, coaching and people management skills
  • Demonstrated ability to communicate, present and influence credibly and effectively at all levels of the organization
